# Meetings end. The work doesn't.

Slatis is a scheduling platform built for teams. Book meetings, route them to the right person automatically, and see your team's real capacity in one place.

[slatis.com](https://slatis.com) &nbsp;·&nbsp; Currently in private beta

---

## What we build

Scheduling tools stop at the booking confirmation. Slatis starts there.

Once a meeting is booked, capacity, workload, and team health update in real time — so you always know what your team can actually take on next. 87% of ops teams report no real visibility into their team's actual workload. Slatis is the fix.

**Capacity Intelligence**
Know what your team can actually take on. Slatis reads committed work, focus time, and load across your team — not just open slots on a calendar.

**Intelligent Routing**
Every incoming booking goes to whoever has real bandwidth. Routing decisions that used to take minutes happen automatically, every time.

**Proactive Visibility**
Spot overloaded members and capacity gaps before they escalate. Insights that surface on their own, without dashboards to build or reports to run.

**Any Calendar, Any Tool**
Connect Google, Microsoft, and Apple calendars in one place. Two-way sync across every provider your team already uses, with API access for custom integrations.
